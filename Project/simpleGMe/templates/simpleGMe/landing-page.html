Mastering the Art of Oracle Tables

Oracle Tables are your best friend in solo TTRPGs. They randomly generate decisions and events, ensuring no two game sessions are the same. Here's how you can use them to keep your adventures fresh and exciting:

  • Think of a question that fits your game's current scenario.
  • Use a dice roll or another random method to get an answer from the Oracle Table.
  • Let the outcome guide the next chapter of your adventure, adding an element of surprise and challenge.
